Private wardrobe planner

ClosetCue

Plan outfits from the wardrobe you already own.

ClosetCue helps you build a private wardrobe catalog, save item photos, and create practical outfit suggestions from your real closet. Choose the day, dress code, weather, color preference, and pieces you want to wear, then review explainable outfit ideas generated on your device.

Your wardrobe stays on your device unless you choose to export a backup.

What it does

One organized place to catalog clothes and plan outfits.

Instead of scrolling through camera-roll photos or trying to remember what works together, ClosetCue lets you add wardrobe items with structured details like category, color, material, fit, formality, season, care need, and weather readiness.

Then use a styling brief to ask for outfits that match the moment.

  • Catalog tops, bottoms, dresses, outerwear, shoes, bags, jewelry, accessories, and hosiery.
  • Save cropped wardrobe item photos.
  • Plan outfits by scenario, dress code, weather, comfort, and color preference.
  • Pin favorite pieces and build outfits around them.
  • Review explanations for why an outfit works.
  • Use the app without an account or cloud wardrobe.

Why it helps

A clearer way to dress from what you already own.

A useful closet is not just a pile of clothes. It is the relationship between pieces: what fits the occasion, what works in rain, what needs ironing, what you wore recently, what feels polished enough for work, and what actually pairs with your shoes.

ClosetCue keeps those details structured so outfit planning can use them.

Backup & restore

Local wardrobe, backup files you choose.

Because ClosetCue keeps data local, backups are important. The app includes backup export and restore tools so you can keep an extra copy of your closet.

A backup file can include wardrobe items, photos, saved outfits, request presets, and preferences. You choose when to create it and where to store it.

Create a .closetcue backup file when you want an extra copy of your wardrobe. Save it somewhere you trust and restore it later if needed.

FAQ

Common questions

Where are my wardrobe records stored?

Your wardrobe items, item photos, notes, saved outfits, request presets, and preferences are stored on your device. Orange Forge AI, LLC does not store your closet records or photos on its servers.

Do I need an account?

No. ClosetCue does not require an account or cloud profile.

How does ClosetCue suggest outfits?

ClosetCue uses an on-device, rule-based styling engine. It scores outfit combinations from your saved wardrobe using structured item details such as color, material, formality, fit, weather readiness, care needs, and availability.

Can I add photos?

Yes. You can use the camera or photo library to add item photos. Added photos are cropped and saved locally by the app.

Why does ClosetCue ask for location?

ClosetCue can use your current location to show local Apple Weather and set weather-related outfit choices. You can deny location access and choose weather manually.

Can I keep a backup?

Yes. ClosetCue lets you export a .closetcue backup file that you control. You choose where to save it and whether to share it.
