App in development

A mole tracking app for careful personal records.

The first planned app from Orange Forge AITM helps people photograph moles, record notes, and compare changes over time from their own iPhone.

The app is being designed around local storage by default. Users can keep a dated visual history on their device, add observations, and choose whether to use an optional AI review for informational context.

Important: This app is for personal tracking and informational support only. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, prevent, or monitor any disease, and it is not a substitute for a qualified healthcare professional.

Product principles

Built around user control.

Local records first

Mole photos, dates, notes, and related details are intended to stay on the user's device unless the user chooses to send selected information elsewhere.

Optional AI review

If enabled, users choose the photos and text sent to an AI provider for a non-medical, informational response. Names, dates of birth, addresses, and similar identifiers are not intended to be sent.

Apple-native backup

Planned backup support will use Apple's iCloud architecture. Orange Forge AITM does not plan to operate user account servers for app data.

Privacy summary

Clear, concise, and aligned with the app design.

The privacy policy now reflects the intended on-device architecture, optional AI sharing, and iCloud backup path. It avoids claims about company servers, user accounts, or broad data collection that are not part of the current plan.
