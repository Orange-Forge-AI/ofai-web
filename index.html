MoleJournal

Track mole records on your own device.

MoleJournal lets you track your moles. You can record measurements and attach photos of your moles, all with an easy-to-use UI to put your moles exactly where they are on a 3D interactive body model. All data are stored on your own device.

Disclaimer: MoleJournal does not diagnose conditions, provide medical advice, or replace care from a qualified healthcare professional. It is purely for personal record keeping.
