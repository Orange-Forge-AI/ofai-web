Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This policy describes the website operated by Orange Forge AI, LLC and the intended privacy design for our mole tracking app currently in development. We will update this policy before release if the app implementation changes, including naming any third-party AI provider used by the app.

1. Scope

Orange Forge AI, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") builds software under the Orange Forge AITM brand. This policy applies to this website and to the planned mole tracking app unless a later in-app policy says otherwise.

2. App data stored on your device

The app is designed to let you store mole photos, dates, notes, location labels, and other tracking details on your own device. We do not currently plan to operate user account servers for storing this app data.

Mole photos and related notes may be sensitive. You control what you record in the app and what you delete from your device.

3. Optional AI review

If the app includes an AI review feature, it will be optional. When you choose to use it, the selected mole photo and related text information you submit will be sent to a third-party AI service for an informational response.

We do not intend to send direct identifiers such as your name, date of birth, address, or account information to the AI provider. The AI provider may still process the photo and submitted text according to its own terms and privacy policy.

AI responses are not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Please contact a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.

4. iCloud backup

We plan to support backup through Apple's native iCloud architecture. If you enable iCloud backup or sync, Apple may process and store your app data under your Apple ID and Apple's privacy terms. We do not control your iCloud account.

5. Website and email contact

This website is a static site. We do not use website accounts, advertising cookies, or analytics scripts. If you email us, we receive your email address and the information you choose to include in your message so we can respond.

The website may be hosted by GitHub Pages. GitHub may process basic technical information, such as server logs, as part of providing hosting.

6. How information is used

We use information you provide to operate the app features you choose, respond to support requests, maintain security, troubleshoot issues, and comply with legal obligations.

7. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share information only when you choose to send data to an AI provider, when data is handled by services you enable such as iCloud, when needed to respond to your support request, or when required by law.

8. Your controls

You can delete app records from your device using the app's controls or your device settings. iCloud backup and deletion controls are managed through your Apple ID and Apple device settings.

To ask a privacy question or request help with information you sent to us by email, contact support@orangeforgeai.com.

9. Children's privacy

Our app and website are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes

We may update this policy as the app moves toward release or as our practices change. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
