Website Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This policy describes the public website operated by Orange Forge AI, LLC. It covers website visits and email contact only. App privacy information is provided on the dedicated app privacy policy page.

1. Scope

This website privacy policy applies to pages hosted for the Orange Forge AITM public website. It does not describe data stored inside MoleJournal or other apps.

2. Static website

This website is a static site. Orange Forge AI, LLC does not use website accounts, advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts on this site.

The website may be hosted by GitHub Pages. GitHub may process basic technical information, such as server logs, IP address, browser type, and request time, as part of providing hosting and security.

3. Email contact

If you email us, we receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message. We use that information to read, route, and respond to your request.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share website contact information only with service providers that help us operate communications, when needed to respond to you, or when required by law.

5. Your choices

You can choose not to email us or can limit the information you include in a message. To ask a website privacy question, contact support@orangeforgeai.com.

6. Children's privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through this website.

7. Changes

We may update this website privacy policy as our website practices change. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
