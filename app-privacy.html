App Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This page provides app privacy information for apps published by Orange Forge AI, LLC. Each app has its own section so future app privacy details can be added here in one place.

Scope

This app privacy policy describes app data practices. It does not replace the website privacy policy, which covers visits to the public website and email contact with Orange Forge AI, LLC.

MoleJournal

Effective date: May 23, 2026

MoleJournal is a personal mole tracking and organization app from Orange Forge AI, LLC. It is designed to store your records on your own device.

Information You Add

You may choose to add profile names, body model selections, mole locations, mole names, photos, measurements, biopsy status, biopsy dates, and biopsy notes.

How Data Is Stored

MoleJournal stores app records and attached photos locally on your device. Orange Forge AI, LLC does not operate a server that receives, stores, or reviews your mole records or photos.

If you export a backup, the app creates a file that you control. You decide where to save it and whether to share it.

Camera And Photo Library

The app may ask for camera or photo library access so you can attach mole photos. Photos are used only for the record you create in the app.

Data Collection

Orange Forge AI, LLC does not collect personal data, mole records, photos, measurements, biopsy notes, analytics, tracking data, or advertising identifiers through MoleJournal.

The app does not sell personal data and does not track you across apps or websites.

Support Requests

If you contact Orange Forge AI, LLC for support, you may choose to provide your name, contact information, device details, screenshots, or a description of your issue. Support information is used only to respond to your request.

Do not send private medical photos or records unless you intentionally choose to include them in a support request.

Medical Disclaimer

MoleJournal is for personal tracking and organization only. It is not a medical device, does not diagnose skin cancer or any other condition, does not analyze photos, and does not replace professional medical care. Contact a dermatologist or other qualified clinician for medical advice.

ClosetCue

Effective date: June 10, 2026

ClosetCue is a wardrobe catalog and outfit planning app from Orange Forge AI, LLC. The app is designed to keep your wardrobe records on your own device.

Information You Add

You may choose to add clothing item names, photos, categories, subcategories, colors, materials, fit, pattern, formality, season, warmth, water resistance, laundry status, care needs, availability, favorite status, and optional notes.

You may also save outfit suggestions, mark outfits worn, save styling request presets, and create backup files.

How Data Is Stored

ClosetCue stores app records and attached wardrobe photos locally on your device. Orange Forge AI, LLC does not operate a server that receives, stores, or reviews your wardrobe items, photos, notes, saved outfits, or request presets.

If you export a backup, the app creates a .closetcue file that you control. You decide where to save it and whether to share it.

Camera And Photo Library

The app may ask for camera or photo library access so you can attach wardrobe item photos. Photos are used only for the item records you create in the app.

Photos added to ClosetCue are cropped and resized before being saved locally by the app.

Location And Weather

ClosetCue may ask for location access to show local Apple Weather and help set weather-related outfit planning choices. If you allow location access, your device location is used to request current weather through Apple's WeatherKit service.

Orange Forge AI, LLC does not store your precise location, add it to your wardrobe records, include it in your backups, or use it for tracking. If you deny location access or local weather is unavailable, you can still choose weather manually when planning outfits.

Data Collection

Orange Forge AI, LLC does not collect wardrobe records, item photos, notes, saved outfits, request presets, backup files, analytics, tracking data, advertising identifiers, or purchase data through ClosetCue.

The app does not sell personal data and does not track you across apps or websites.

Support Requests

If you contact Orange Forge AI, LLC for support, you may choose to provide your name, contact information, device details, screenshots, or a description of your issue. Support information is used only to respond to your request.

Do not send private wardrobe photos, backup files, or personal records unless you intentionally choose to include them in a support request.

Contact And Updates

For questions about app privacy, contact support@orangeforgeai.com.

Orange Forge AI, LLC may update this app privacy policy as app practices change or as additional apps are published.
