App Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This page provides app privacy information for apps published by Orange Forge AI, LLC. Each app has its own section so future app privacy details can be added here in one place.

Scope

This app privacy policy describes app data practices. It does not replace the website privacy policy, which covers visits to the public website and email contact with Orange Forge AI, LLC.

MoleJournal

Effective date: May 23, 2026

MoleJournal is a personal mole tracking and organization app from Orange Forge AI, LLC. It is designed to store your records on your own device.

Information You Add

You may choose to add profile names, body model selections, mole locations, mole names, photos, measurements, biopsy status, biopsy dates, and biopsy notes.

How Data Is Stored

MoleJournal stores app records and attached photos locally on your device. Orange Forge AI, LLC does not operate a server that receives, stores, or reviews your mole records or photos.

If you export a backup, the app creates a file that you control. You decide where to save it and whether to share it.

Camera And Photo Library

The app may ask for camera or photo library access so you can attach mole photos. Photos are used only for the record you create in the app.

Data Collection

Orange Forge AI, LLC does not collect personal data, mole records, photos, measurements, biopsy notes, analytics, tracking data, or advertising identifiers through MoleJournal.

The app does not sell personal data and does not track you across apps or websites.

Support Requests

If you contact Orange Forge AI, LLC for support, you may choose to provide your name, contact information, device details, screenshots, or a description of your issue. Support information is used only to respond to your request.

Do not send private medical photos or records unless you intentionally choose to include them in a support request.

Medical Disclaimer

MoleJournal is for personal tracking and organization only. It is not a medical device, does not diagnose skin cancer or any other condition, does not analyze photos, and does not replace professional medical care. Contact a dermatologist or other qualified clinician for medical advice.

Contact And Updates

For questions about app privacy, contact support@orangeforgeai.com.

Orange Forge AI, LLC may update this app privacy policy as app practices change or as additional apps are published.
