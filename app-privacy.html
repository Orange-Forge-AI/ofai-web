MoleJournal

MoleJournal is designed for on-device record keeping. Mole locations, measurements, notes, and attached photos are stored on your own device.

The app does not require an account with Orange Forge AI, LLC or a company server to store your mole records. If you choose to share, export, back up, or sync information using device or operating system features, those services handle that information under their own terms and privacy settings.

MoleJournal does not diagnose conditions, provide medical advice, or replace care from a qualified healthcare professional. It is purely for personal record keeping.
